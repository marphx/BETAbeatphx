how it’s done

Choose a bank, tap a pad, and build your loop. Use Predetermined Measures when you want a set loop size, or use Freeform Loop and tap REC again exactly when you want the loop to close. You can also record your voice, beatbox, rap, sing, or sample a short audio snippet into any pad from your phone microphone.

REC
Tap once to arm standby. The first pad hit starts the recording. If the loop is already running, REC turns overdub on or off.
Quick Controls
Choose Pad FX to affect the selected pad, or Master Mix to affect the full beat.
Loop Length
Fixed loops can run long and stay stable. Use Loop Bars and Extend Loop if you want more room without breaking playback.
Export
Name your beat first, then export to MP3. If the MP3 encoder is blocked, the app will save a WAV fallback instead.
