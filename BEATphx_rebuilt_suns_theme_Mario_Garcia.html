how it’s done

Pick a sound bank, tap the six pads, and build your loop. Use Predetermined Measures for a set bar length, or Freeform Loop to close the loop exactly when you tap REC again. You can also record your voice, beatbox, rap, sing, or capture a small audio snippet onto any pad with the mic sampler.

1. Sound Banks
Switch banks anytime. Recorded sounds stay in your loop, and new hits use the currently selected bank.
2. Record + Overdub
Tap REC once to arm standby. Your first pad hit starts the loop. When the loop is running, tap REC to turn overdub on or off.
3. Quick Controls
Choose whether the sliders affect the selected pad or the full mix. Tap a pad first to target that pad.
4. Export
Name your beat, then export an MP3. If the MP3 encoder is unavailable, the app can still save a WAV backup.
